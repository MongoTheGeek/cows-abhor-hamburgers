Cows Abhor Hamburgers

Cows Abhor Hamburgers is an unofficial expansion for Cards Against Humanity. These templates can be used to create custom cards that are suitable for professional printing.

The resulting cards are 2.5 x 3.5 inches. The template includes margin and bleed areas to ensure all text appears on the final card.
